Activities

RECREATION

With a swimming pool, hiking, biking, a 24-hour fitness center, beach volleyball, basketball and tennis courts, the sun isn’t the only thing on Peter Island that will make you sweat. Complimentary morning yoga is also offered Sunday - Thursday at 9:30 am, in the Tranquility Garden at the spa

WATERSPORTS

Enjoy our array of non-motorized water sports, including kayaks, Hobie Cats, windsurfers, Sunfish sailboats and paddleboards. And if you’re not quite sure how to use the equipment, we are happy to provide a free lesson.

Sunset at The Loop

From 5pm­ to 6:30pm every evening, watch a true sunset from the most beautiful point on the Island. We drop you off with a fruit and cheese platter and your favorite beverage and leave you to watch the sunset over the stunning backdrop.

PICNIC LUNCH

Guests may spend the whole day on White Bay or Honeymoon beaches by placing a picnic lunch order at the front office the day before.

PRIVATE DINNER ON THE BEACH

Imagine just the two of you under the stars at a candlelight table by the water’s edge. A private server will take care of your every wish
